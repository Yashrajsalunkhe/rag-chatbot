Q: What is LangChain?
A: LangChain is a framework for developing applications powered by language models.

Q: What is RAG?
A: Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) combines external knowledge with language models to give better answers.

Q: What is FAISS?
A: FAISS is a library for efficient similarity search and clustering of dense vectors.

Q: What is HuggingFace?
A: HuggingFace provides free and open access to transformer models for NLP.

Q: How does a chatbot work?
A: A chatbot takes user input, processes it using a model, and returns a response.
